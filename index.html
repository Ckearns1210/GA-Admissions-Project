Colin Kearns

Colin Kearns is an aspiring Front-End Web Developer in New Orleans, Louisiana.

Colin was born in Manchester, New Hampshire, but grew up largely in Northern New Jersey. He attended Tulane University in New Orleans, Louisiana, where he studied Political Economy. His sophomore year brought Hurricane Katrina, a subsequent semester at Boston University, and a deep appreciation for the resiliency of the community of New Orleans.

After graduating college, Colin stayed in the city he had come to love, working both as a paralegal and a waiter. More recently, he has discovered a passion for the workings of the web, and decided to take the steps necessary to turn this passion into a career. In his free time, when not serving Thai Food, Colin enjoys eating Sno-Balls and listening to experimental music.
